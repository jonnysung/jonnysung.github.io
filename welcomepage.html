Which Starters do I pick?

As any newcomer has and would wonder, which out of the 3 starting pokemon should I pick and which one is the right one for me? Well look no further as you are sure to find your answer here. In versions firered and leafgreen the starters that you can choose from are the water type Squirtle, the grass type Bulbasaur, and the fire type Charmander. Firstly I would like to state that no matter which stater you choose, you will be able to experience the game to the maximum and will not hinder your progress. However, if you are a beginner, I would personally recommend starting with Charmander. Fire types always tend to be a strong choice in any generation and you cannot go wrong with choosing one. Furthermore, Charizard, Charmander's final evolution, is an extremely strong fire/flying type pokemon that can go toe to toe with even the strongest legendary pokemon. Moves such as dragon claw, fly, flare blitz, and mega punch all give very good coverage against weaknesses while still capitalizing on Charizard's strong stats.
